BrandBox Security Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At BrandBox, we are committed to ensuring that your shopping experience is safe and secure. This policy outlines the measures we take to protect your data and how you can stay safe while shopping online.

1. Secure Browsing (SSL)

Our website uses Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology to encrypt the data transmitted between your browser and our servers. This ensures that your personal information, like names and addresses, is kept private.

2. Payment Security

We do not store your credit card or debit card information on our servers. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant secure payment gateways to ensure maximum protection against fraud.

3. Local Storage Safety

We use LocalStorage to enhance your experience by saving your cart and product selections. This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible by other websites.

  • Your cart data is stored only to keep your session active.
  • We recommend clearing your browser cache if you are using a public computer.

4. Protecting Your Account

If you create an account with us, you are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password. We recommend using a unique password that is not used on other websites.

5. Phishing Protection

BrandBox will never ask for your password or sensitive payment details via email or SMS. If you receive any suspicious requests, please do not click any links and report it to us immediately.

Report a Security Issue

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability on our website, please report it to our security team at: security@brandbox.com

We appreciate your help in keeping the BrandBox community safe!
