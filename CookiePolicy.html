BrandBox Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At BrandBox, we believe in being clear and open about how we collect and use data related to you. This policy provides detailed information about how and when we use cookies on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website in the following ways:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function (e.g., keeping you logged in or saving items in your cart).
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our site, allowing us to fix errors and improve design.
  • Functionality Cookies: These remember your preferences, such as your language or region settings.

3. Local Storage & Session Data

In addition to cookies, we use LocalStorage to save your shopping cart data. This allows your selected items to remain in the cart even if you refresh the page or close your browser tab.

4. How to Manage Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies, but please note that this may affect the functionality of our website (e.g., you might not be able to store items in your cart correctly).

Need More Help?

If you have any questions regarding our use of cookies or other technologies, please email us at: cookies@brandbox.com

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
