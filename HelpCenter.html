Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will send you an email with a tracking number and a link to the courier's website. You can also check your status in the 'My Account' section.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for all unused items with their original tags. Simply visit our Returns page to initiate a request.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, Net Banking, and popular digital wallets. Cash on Delivery (COD) is also available for select locations.

How long does delivery take?

Typically, orders are delivered within 3-5 business days. Remote areas might take up to 7 business days.

Why are my items disappearing from the cart?

We use LocalStorage to keep your cart active. If you clear your browser's cache or use Incognito mode, your cart data may be lost.
