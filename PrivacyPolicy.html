BrandBox Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Welcome to BrandBox. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by BrandBox and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our store or make a purchase, we collect certain information to provide a better experience:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Data: We do not store credit card details; payments are handled through secure gateways.
  • Browsing Data: Information about the products you view and add to your cart.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders.
  • Improve and personalize your shopping experience.
  • Communicate with you regarding order updates.
  • Prevent fraudulent transactions and enhance security.

3. Local Storage Usage

BrandBox uses LocalStorage to save your cart items and selected products temporarily on your browser. This ensures that you don't lose your shopping progress when navigating between pages.

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (like image hosting or analytics tools). These parties have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle data.
